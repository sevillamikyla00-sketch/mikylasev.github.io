About DCCP

Who We Are

Data Center College of the Philippines (DCCP) is dedicated to providing excellence in technical and academic education. We focus on developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to meet the demands of the global workforce through relevant instruction and community engagement.

Â 

History of DCCP

Origin: Data Center College of the Philippines was founded in **1974 in Davao City**, starting with keypunching and basic computer training. Expansion: In **1980, DCCP established a branch in Baguio City**, reorienting its curriculum to include courses on microcomputers. Abra Campus: The DCCP - Bangued Campus in Abra is part of this broader educational network, committed to local development through quality education. Its mission is to nurture students' physical, socio-cultural, and moral potential while providing quality and relevant education for total development.

Future Focus and Growth

DCCP envisions becoming a leading institution in the Philippines, producing graduates who contribute to local, regional, and national development and are globally competitive.

Core Development Principles

DCCP is committed to the holistic development of students and providing relevant education and skills so they can competently pursue their chosen professions with honor.
